Manner and Habits of France

  • Dining Etiquette

    It is good manners to eat with a fork in your left hand and a knife in your right.

  • unpunctual

    It is normal to be later than the scheduled time. When eating lunch, the French can be about three hours late.

  • Shopping

    In France, there are no 24-hour stores like convenience stores. The market is closed on Sundays.
